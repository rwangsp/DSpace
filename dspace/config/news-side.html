APRIL 2019 - Take advantage of TSpace assisted deposit for Tri-Agency Open Access Policy compliance

TSpace team continues offering assisted deposit service to help U of T affiliated CIHR, SSHRC, and NSERC grant recipients with meeting the requirements of the Tri-Agency Open Access Policy on Publications. This policy applies to all grants awarded by NSERC or SSHRC from May 1, 2015 and onward; and by CIHR from January 1, 2008 and onward.

To take advantage of the assisted deposit service, please email us with:

  1. Accepted manuscript versions* of your grant-funded publications, and
  2. Signed Mediated Deposit Agreement

*Publisher copyright policies are more likely to permit the deposit of an accepted manuscript rather than the final article version. See our Published Paper Versions guide or contact us to help identify the version you need.

The TSpace team will verify publisher permissions, process and deposit materials to TSpace, and provide you with a permanent link to you publications. Processing times are subject to our workload and wait times associated with contacting publishers for permissions.

If you have questions about the service or Tri-Agency Open Access Policy compliance, feel welcome to contact us.

DECEMBER 2018 - Launch of the real time usage map and improved stats display

We are excited to offer you a real time view of TSpace items downloaded across the globe. The homepage map is updated every few seconds to reflect the current usage.

We have also improved stats display for individual items, collections, and communities. You will now be able to see the last 6 months trend of page views and file downloads for individual items as well as in aggregate at collection and community levels.

Finally, we have enabled Altmetric badges - you will see them displayed on items that have Altmetric score.

As always, if you need custom stats about your item or collection, or have any questions, feel welcome to contact us.
